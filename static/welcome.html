Welcome to ImmPort Galaxy!

Welcome to a new release of ImmPort Galaxy!
The Galaxy framework was upgraded to 16.07 which now enables format auto-detection of your files upon upload. We have also added a couple of tools to collapse populations automatically based on the quartile binning of FLOCK results, and to generate summary statistics of flowtext files.
Last but not least, check out a new graph option in the visualization tools: boxplots!

What is ImmPort?

Search and download shared biomedical research data funded from NIAID DAIT and DMID, and other NIH and non-government funding agencies on OpenImmPort. Additional resources available include step-by-step data reuse tutorials including example R and Python analysis code, the Cell Ontology Visualizer, the Cytokine Registry, and ImmuneXpresso - the cytokine and cell interaction literature mining tool.

ImmPort Galaxy is powered by the Galaxy Project.
